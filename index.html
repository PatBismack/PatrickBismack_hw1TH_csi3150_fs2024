My name is Patrick Bismack, I am a senior at Oakland University majoring in computer science. I am from Rochester Hills, MI, but I currently live in Ann Arbor, MI. In addition to my work, I hold a Google Cloud Associate Cloud Engineer certification and have spoken at a conference on integrating LLM into software development. In the future, I want to work as a software engineering manager. If you would like to find out more about me check my resume and fun facts.
